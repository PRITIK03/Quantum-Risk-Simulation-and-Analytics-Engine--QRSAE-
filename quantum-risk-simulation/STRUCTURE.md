# Quantum Risk Analyst Project Structure & Timeline

Based on the analysis of **291307A_Quantum_Computing_Risk_Analyst_for_Banks.pdf**, here is the required structure and build plan.

## 1. Timeline & "When to Build"
The project is designed as a **15-Day Innovation Sprint**.

*   **Days 1-3: Discovery & Setup**
    *   Initialize the Simulation Dashboard (Prototype built).
    *   Research "Tier 1" Banking Assets (Simulated in app).
    *   Deliverable: Part A (Research) & Part B (Business Concept).
*   **Days 4-10: The "Build" (Simulation Logic)**
    *   Implement the core "Game Loop": Scan Assets -> Identify Vulnerability -> Select Vendor -> Migrate.
    *   Simulate the "Q-Day" countdown mechanics.
*   **Days 11-15: Polish & Documentation**
    *   Finalize the "350-Page" documentation package (Simulated or drafted).
    *   Create the 5-7 minute Video Demo.
    *   Prepare the 20-slide Presentation.

## 2. Proper Folder Structure (Submission)
You must organize your deliverables into the following folder hierarchy:

```
/Submission_Package
  /Part A - Research & Context
      - 1_Quantum_Threat_Landscape.pdf
  /Part B - Business Concept
      - 2_Risk_Analyst_Role_Definition.pdf
  /Part C - Case Studies
      - 3_Historical_Breaches.pdf
  /Part D - Methodology (The Core 15-Day Sprint)
      - 4_Daily_Logs.pdf
      - 5_Decision_Matrix.xlsx
  /Part E - The Tool (Simulation)
      - /Source_Code (or No-Code link)
      - Game_Design_Document.pdf
      - User_Manual.pdf
  /Part F - Assessment & Strategy
      - 6_Strategic_Impact_Analysis.pdf
      - 7_Patent_Strategy.pdf
```
